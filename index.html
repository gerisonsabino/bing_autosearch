Tutorial
  1. Sign in to Bing with your Microsoft Rewards account and return to this page.
  2. Start a new auto search by clicking the "Start Auto Search" button, the progress counter will be showned.
  3. To perform mobile searches, use your smartphone or emulate a mobile browser through the developer tools (F12) on the PC.
