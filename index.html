Auto Search Tutorial
  1. Use Microsoft Edge () to earn extra points.
  2. Sign in to Bing with your Microsoft Rewards account and return to this page.
  3. Start a new auto search by clicking the "Start Auto Search" button (limit of 50 per click).
  4. The auto search progress counter can be viewed on the "Stop Auto Search" button.
  5. To perform mobile searches, use your smartphone or emulate a mobile browser through the developer tools (F12) on the PC.
Multi-tab Mode

Due to system restrictions, for some devices/browsers it may be necessary to open multiple tabs to receive points (if this is your case, check the box below and try again).

Attention! To use the multi-tab mode, it is necessary to allow the opening of multiple tabs/pop-ups in your browser.
